As a part of CS4All NYC, I teach 6th graders Computer Science/Coding using p5.js with the Creative Web curriculum. There is also a similar high school curriculum about p5.js, Introduction to Computational Media (ICM). I have co-facilitated CS4All NYC professional development about both Creative Web and ICM.

Desmos Activties for Students

To support my students with expressing themselves with p5, I have written some Desmos activities to help them understand the p5 coordinate grid and visualize the basic shapes and place them in their p5 sketches. Desmos Activity Builder does require you to sign up to use activties, but it is free. I've also presented at TeachersCon 2022 about these Desmos activties: presentation slides
